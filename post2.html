Top 15 healthy food

1. AVOCADOS

1/2 avocado=114 calories

Benefits: Has twice as much potassium as a banana. The healthy monounsaturated fats can help lower cholesterol levels. Babies could live on avocado due to the high fat content can helps with normal infant growth and development.

1 medium=75 calories

Benefits: Contains antioxidants called flavonoids,which may help lower risk of developing diabetes and asthma. They are high in pectin. They help eliminate toxic build up and cleanse the intestine.

Benefits: They fight infections,lower blood pressure,reduce menstrual pains,help deliver oxygen to the brain ,boast circulation,reduce stroke risk and fight obesity.They are anti-diarrheal.


4. STRAWBERRY
