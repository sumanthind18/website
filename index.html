Top 7 tips to stay healthy


There are hundreds of tips you can do to stay healthy. Here are my top 7

  1. Eat a balanced diet
  2. Stay active whether it's cardio, working out at the gym or yoga
  3. Keep your brain active through reading or taking a course
  4. Take care of your mental health
  5. Try and go to bed and wake up around the same time every day
  6. Don't be too hard on yourself. Change is hard, the key is babysteps
  7. Spend time with good friends. Don't forget pets too
