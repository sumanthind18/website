Benefits of eating healthy



“Eat food. Not too much. Mostly plants.”
— Michael Pollan

1. Added Energy

It should'nt come as a surprise that eating healthy food filled with nutrients give you energy. Often, starting to eat healthy can offer your body more energy than you hav eever had before.

2. Better Digestion

When you eat the right amount of fibre, cutu back on greasy food, limit your sugar intake, your digestion improves significantly. You will likely experience less heart burn and gas after changing your diet

3. Happier Mood

When you eat right you feel good. Good food can help your mood improve because you won't feel all of the usual energy slumps during the day

4. Reduced Risk

When you eat right, you automatically reduce your risk of hearrt disease, stroke, and some cancers. However, you should continue taking any prescribed medicine you have been given for these.

5. Mental Health

Healthy food provides a stronger, faster brain functioning and more energy to get through work or your busy day. Studies have shown that students who a complete breakfast before school are more focussed on class.
