Medical Conditions

By maintaining proper diet, sleep and exercise you can prevent

  1. Risk of Type 2 diabetes
  2. Risk of hypertension (or high blood pressure)
  3. High cholesterol
  4. Poor mental health
  5. Poor Sleep
  6. Stress
  7. Decreased bone density
  8. Risk of early death

"Laughing, listening to music, meditating, alternate nostril breathing, exercising and eating properly, are all ways to reduce daily stress. Because who doesn't need a little help with that in the stressful 9to5 society we live in."

Preventing diseases due to poor health choices can be easy if you want it to be. Between getting to bed on time every night, eating a balanced diet and exercising daily, you can help prevent most health related diseases.
